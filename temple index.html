About the Temple

Location & History

Nestled in the spiritual heart of the city, Shri Arthanareeswarar Temple has been a beacon of faith for over a century. Our temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, and serves as a spiritual sanctuary for devotees from all walks of life.

Address: South Street, Ulagiyanallur, Kallakurichi, Tamil Nadu - 606204, India

Pre-marriage Registration

Begin your marital journey with divine blessings. Our temple offers special pre-marriage ceremonies and pujas to sanctify your union according to Vedic traditions.

Registration Process:

  • Register at least 7 days in advance

  • Choose from auspicious Muhurtha dates shown in calendar
  • Consult with temple priest for personalized rituals
  • Ceremony duration: 2-3 hours

Temple Guidelines

Important Notes & Rules

Temple Etiquette

  • Remove footwear before entering
  • Maintain silence in sanctum
  • Dress modestly (covered shoulders & knees)
  • Mobile phones on silent mode

Photography Rules

  • Allowed only in designated areas
  • No flash photography
  • No photography during rituals
  • Respect privacy of other devotees

Prohibited Items

  • Non-vegetarian food
  • Alcohol & tobacco products
  • Plastic bags & wrappers
  • Large bags & luggage
